Meet Your CA

CA Shashank K. S. Raikar

ACA 2022

Precision. Reliability. Excellence.

Associate Chartered Accountant · 10+ Years Experience · 3 Years in Practice

I'm CA Shashank K. S. Raikar, a practicing Chartered Accountant based in Margao, Goa. After qualifying as an ACA in 2022, I've dedicated my practice to helping businesses achieve seamless compliance and financial clarity.

I have been running my professional office since 2016, initially as a tax consultant, and have been actively handling GST matters since its introduction in July 2017. With over a decade of experience in accounting and taxation, I have developed strong expertise in GST compliance, income tax planning, and financial management.

Over the years, I have worked with a wide range of clients across industries — from small family-run businesses to organisations with turnovers exceeding ₹200 crores. This diverse exposure enables me to provide practical, business-focused solutions tailored to each client's needs.

What sets our practice apart is our commitment to personalised service backed by a structured team approach. Every client receives dedicated attention while benefiting from the efficiency of our expert team.

Our Services

Comprehensive compliance solutions for every business need

📊 GST Compliance

Comprehensive GST services for 100+ clients across diverse industries. We handle monthly GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B filings, quarterly returns for composition scheme taxpayers, annual GSTR-9 reconciliations, and input tax credit optimisation.

  • Monthly GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B filing
  • Quarterly composition scheme returns
  • Annual GSTR-9 and reconciliation statements
  • Input Tax Credit optimisation and claims
  • GST registration and amendments
  • E-Invoice and E-way bill compliance

💼 Income Tax Filings

Expert tax return preparation for individuals, professionals, and businesses. We ensure accurate documentation, tax planning, and timely filing for complete peace of mind.

  • Individual and business ITR filing
  • TDS compliance and quarterly returns
  • Tax planning and advisory
  • Advance tax computation and payment
  • Capital gains calculations
  • Form 16/16A preparation
